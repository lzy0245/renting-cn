# Contract And Payment Risk

Use this reference for lease review, signing preparation, authority checks, sublet risks, deposit risks, and move-out disputes. This is not legal advice.

## Authority And Parties

Clarify before signing:

- Who is the actual owner, authorized agent, apartment operator, current tenant, roommate, or second landlord.
- Whether the signer has ownership certificate, authorization letter, original lease, or written sublet consent as applicable.
- Whether the lease party matches the rent/deposit payee.
- Whether the contract includes the exact address, room, shared areas, and allowed use.

High risk:

- Refusal to show authority proof before signing.
- Sublet without written landlord consent.
- Payee differs from signer with no written explanation.
- The user is asked to pay a "holding deposit" before authority and terms are clear.

## Money Terms

Confirm in writing:

- Rent amount, deposit amount, payment cycle, due date, late fee, and receipt/transfer note.
- Agency fee, service fee, management fee, property fee, cleaning fee, internet, utilities, parking, and other mandatory charges.
- Utility rate: residential or commercial, meter access, calculation method, and settlement date.
- What deposit can be deducted for, who decides, evidence needed, and refund deadline.

Avoid vague phrases such as "deduct according to actual situation" without standards.

## Repairs And Condition

Write down:

- Existing defects and item condition at handover.
- Who repairs appliances, plumbing, leaks, locks, AC/heating, water heater, and internet.
- Response time and cost responsibility for non-user-caused damage.
- Whether promised repairs or replacements must be completed before move-in or by a stated date.

## Early Termination And Renewal

Check:

- Minimum lease term and notice period.
- Whether early termination forfeits deposit, requires replacement tenant, or pays penalty.
- Whether subletting or lease transfer is allowed.
- Renewal notice, rent increase, renewal fee, and priority rules.

## Move-Out And Deposit Evidence

Prepare:

- Contract, payment records, repair records, chat records, move-in handover list, photos/videos, and meter readings.
- Written move-out notice according to the contract.
- Full move-out video and handover list.
- Written explanation for each deduction item and its basis.

If a dispute arises, separate:

- Facts.
- Evidence.
- Contract clause.
- Demand.
- Deadline for response.
- Escalation path such as platform complaint, local mediation, consumer hotline, housing authority, or legal consultation when appropriate.

## Suggested Clause Prompts

Use prompts like:

```text
请把以下内容写进合同或补充协议：具体房号、租金、押金、付款周期、全部费用、家具家电清单、已有损坏、维修责任、提前退租规则、押金扣除标准、退押金时间、签约方和收款方关系。
```
